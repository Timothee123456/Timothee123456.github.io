Cambodia facts

The population of cambodia is 16 million people! It has a very young population with around one-third of it younger than 15 years old!

Cambodia is a mostly flat country with its flat plains, apart from some small mountains.

Cambodia has lots of temples. One is the largest religious monument in the world: Angkor Wat.

Cambodia changes its name for every new government for the past 60 years! It started and ended with “The Kingdom of Cambodia”.

Tonlé Sap is the only river in the world that flows in two different directions in a year!

There are 4 to 6 millions of active landmines still scattered across the country from its civil war!

Surprisingly, there has never been a McDonald in Cambodia!

Cambodia’s main transportation is Tuk-tuks.

The main agriculture in cambodia is rice.

The main food in cambodia is fish.
